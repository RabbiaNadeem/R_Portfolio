## DESIGN NOTES FOR CYBER-SOFT AESTHETIC

**Color Palette Suggestions**:
- Primary Dark: `#0A0E27` (deep navy)
- Accent holographic gradient: `#00D9FF` (cyan) → `#9D4EDD` (purple)
- Soft pastel accent: `#FF006E` (neon pink) or `#3A86FF` (sky blue)
- Text: `#E0E7FF` (soft lavender white)

**Typography**:
- Headers: Bold, geometric sans-serif (e.g., Inter, Outfit)
- Body: Light to regular weight for that "soft" feel
- Use subtle gradient text for section titles

**Interactive Elements**:
- Smooth hover transitions with glow effects
- StarField or particle effects in background (as you already have)
- Glassmorphism for cards: `backdrop-filter: blur(10px)`
- Subtle animations on scroll
